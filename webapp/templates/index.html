Syntax Guide

        
    
          
  • Use sin(x), cos(x), tan(x), exp(x), log(x), etc.
    • 
          
  • Constants: pi (π), E (e)
    • 
          
  • Exponentiation: ** (e.g. x**2), not ^
    • 
          
  • Multiplication must be explicit: 2*x, not 2x
    • 
          
  • Parentheses for grouping: (x+1)/(x-1)
    • 
          
  • Absolute value: Abs(x)
    • 
          
  • Piecewise: Piecewise((x, x < 0),(2*x, True))
    •
